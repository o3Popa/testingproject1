Module 1: Understanding Markets

Lesson 1: What is a Market?

Comprehensive definition of a market, key components, types of markets, and an interactive simulation to categorize different markets.

Topics Covered:

  • Comprehensive definition of a market.
  • Key components: buyers, sellers, goods/services, exchange mechanisms.
  • Types of markets: physical vs. virtual, primary vs. secondary.

Interactive Simulation:

Users categorize different markets based on provided characteristics within the simulation environment. Analyze and identify core components of a chosen market (e.g., local farmers' market, online marketplace) using the simulation tool.

Module 2: How Markets are Generated

Lesson 2: How Markets Get Created

Explore the fundamental needs for exchange in society, the role of supply and demand, and how economic factors influence market formation.

Topics Covered:

  • The fundamental need for exchange in society.
  • Supply and demand as the foundation of market creation.
  • Economic factors at an individual level influencing market formation.

Interactive Simulation:

Users model market creation by adjusting supply and demand parameters and observing outcomes.
